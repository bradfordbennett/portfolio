A series of musical canons written to explore simplified, slow sonic structures. Noise is used to heighten and at times obscure the tonal elements, pulling clarity in and out of focus. Many of the tracks were made in collaboration with Mx Robert Frost. The title nods to Ornette Coleman and The Refused, artists whose forward-looking use of noise and chaos helped develop their singular visions. Released by Art As Catharsis in Australia and Inner Demons Records in the US.
