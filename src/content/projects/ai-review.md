*AI Review* was a multimedia installation at Ellipsis Gallery, Woolloomooloo.

The work combined sound art, cassette tape loops, and print pieces exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and human creativity.

**Exhibition:** AI Review at Ellipsis Gallery, Sydney
