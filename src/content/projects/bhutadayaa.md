<div stylemargin: 2rem 0;>

[The Incognito Art Show](https://www.incognitoartshow.com/) is a non-profit that sells artworks for $100. The artist remains anonymous until the work is purchased.

</div>

<div stylemargin: 2rem 0;>

For the 2024 Incognito Art Show I gave two pieces that weren't primarily visual art. If you bought one of my canvases, you became the owner of a song that no one other than me has ever heard. The password on the back of the canvas allowed the new owner to download and take over all rights. I also asked the new owner to contact me if they wanted me to share with the rest of the world.

</div>

<div stylemargin: 2rem 0;>

Both my pieces sold. Only one person contacted me. They decided to name their new track Bhutadayaa and let me share online. They explained about the name: It's Sanskrit for universal benevolence/compassion for all living beings.

</div>

<div stylemargin: 2rem 0;>

Awesomely, the new owner was an artist that incorporated the track into their work. Bhutadayaa featured in an art bomb at the 2024 Venice Biennale.

</div>
